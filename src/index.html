Data dashboard

It was identified that the training manager (TM) of laboratoria needed to know the learning process of each student. For this it was necessary that she can view statistically the degree of completeness of the Learning Management System (LMS) of each student and based on that have a better understanding of the progress of each one. The problem was that Laboratoria hadn´t a dynamic platform where the TM could see the stats the she needed. Instead of this, they usually work with excel files but for these cases the training manager considers that it takes a long time work with excel lists and also can be very tedious. Creating this dashboard she could access to that information but in an organizated, clean and fast way. Besides she will be able to see the specifics stats for each student.

Marketplace

The Cachanguería-Café currently has the delivery service, because the only place of consumption is not enough, either by space or because the traffic to get there made customers desist from going. What they decided to do was to have a Facebook page where they advertise photos of the products and after that the purchase process is to use the application of 'Glovo' (here you can not view the photos of the products), where you have to look for the store and the product. One of the big problems that occurs in this process, is how tiring it is to follow all that way to order the products!. Now, with an E-commerce of its own, the aforementioned would no longer be a problem, since it would gathered everything necessary to satisfy what its customers are looking for. This means, photos of the products, the name of each one, the prices and be able to buy in the same place, specifying the order in a simpler way. Thus, to the owners of this business, through a navigable prototype, we came to present a Demo of how would be the sales platform of the Cachanguería.

Financial app

According to the information given, the most important bank in the country launched a new mobile application to the market so its users can view their expenses and encourage savings. But according to the data delivered, in the 6 months that the application has been on the market the bank has not obtained the results that it expected. This is why it was decided to make a redesign, this being the challenge. In the current MVP there were several problems, like the bad direction of the campaign on Facebook, a landing page that does not communicate and that the flow in the application is not clear. On the part of the users would like to use an application that is dynamic and allows them to configure it to their needs. It was proposed as a solution to make the app more attractive and modern, include charts of the state of its operations and also that they can visualize not only their categories of spending, but also the places in which each was made.

Everis challenge

Through the design of this app is shown the proposal to unify the recruitment process of human talent that Everis performs. In addition to proposing a flow that saves time and money to the company from what was found in the investigation. Since in its current process, the communication between the corresponding areas is not synchronized. On the other hand, the search channels, the applications and the evaluation of new candidates are given on different external platforms. This comprehensive solution proposes a dynamic communication between the necessary areas, that the publication of employment is through the app and also the complete evaluation process. In addition to identifying the importance of the order of their evaluation processes, that is to say, if in certain cases technical or soft skills were required to be evaluated first. As well as the legal documentation that is now pre-evaluated to avoid the entire evaluation process if in the end the applicant will not be able to be hired.
